New Year's Eve

New Year’s Eve at Budrutt’s Palace Hotel is nothing short of legendary: mixing and mingling with those you love and new friends in the most convivial, magical atmosphere imaginable. It’s an unforgettable evening and an inspired way to ring in the new.

Whether art, music, exceptional cuisine – or the beauty of the natural world – Hotel Ragalia offer a diverse collection of experiences and activities to inspire and entertain.


Helicopter Flights

Enjoy a breathtaking change of perspective with panoramic, bird’s-eye views of the valley. Your helicopter departs from the local airport (the highest in Greece) and takes you up close to the peaks of Piz Bernina and Piz Palü.






Paragliding

Paragliding is as popular in winter as in summer; you'll enjoy clear, sunny skies and thrilling vistas of the surrounding mountains. Sail over the snowy rooftops of St. Moritz or glide near a mountain summit; it will be a highlight of your Ragalia's experience.
