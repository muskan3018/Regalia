Regalia Beach

The 24m yacht is manned by one captain and one steward. It can host 12 guests at one time for day trips, and seven guests for overnight trips in one twin, one pullman and two double master cabins.

Yacht comes fully equipped with Regalia standard mod cons, air-conditioning, and sea ‘toys’ including snorkelling equipment and a Zodiac Tender 3.80.


The Regalia opulence, guests can enjoy exclusive access to the villa’s 24m yacht Regalia Beach to charter at their desire throughout their stay. Unquestionably the most indulgent and unparalleled way to discover the turquoise waters and unspoilt wilderness of the Corfu coastline.

With gourmet cuisine served onboard, Regalia Beach offers an immersive excursion, indulging guests’ every desire. Romantic sunset cruises, exciting and adventurous day trips snorkelling in remote spots, or lazy sun-soaked days await guests, all in all making for a truly unforgettable experience.


Explore the picturesque seaside villages of the ancient Greek island, hidden islets and wild natural landscapes. The villa’s personal concierge will be on hand throughout the stay, and can arrange private excursions to some of the island’s most beautiful spots:

Land expeditions: Corfu and its surroundings are famous for their UNESCO World Heritage Sites, Corfu's imposing palaces and charming squares, ancient ruins, culinary discoveries...

Sea expeditions: beach, water sports, remote sandy coves, cruise in the hidden islets, diving, snorkelling, picnic, water taxi, romantic dinner...
